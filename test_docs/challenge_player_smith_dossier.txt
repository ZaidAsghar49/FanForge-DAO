Executive Summary:
Steven Smith is widely regarded as one of the finest red-ball batsmen of his generation. This report details his statistical achievements in Test and One-Day International (ODI) matches, evaluating his baseline averages, strike rates across conditions, and matchups against different bowling styles.

Section 1: Test Match Mastery
Steven Smith's unorthodox technique has proven remarkably resilient in the longest format of the game.
Over his career, Steven Smith's batting average in Test matches is 43.4. This high average reflects his ability to play long innings and anchor the Australian batting order.
Furthermore, his scoring rate is measured and deliberate. Steven Smith's strike rate in Test matches is 51.8. This reflects his approach of wearing down the opposition bowlers over time.
In terms of peak performances, Steven Smith's high score in Test matches is 211. This double century stands as a testament to his concentration and endurance.
His performance is equally strong away from home conditions. For example, Steven Smith's batting average in Test matches in Away conditions is 47.1. This exceptional away record highlights his adaptability to diverse pitches worldwide.

Section 2: Bowling Matchups in Test Cricket
Against spin bowlers, he has been particularly dominant. Steven Smith's batting average against Spin bowling in Test matches is 60.5. His quick footwork and ability to read the ball out of the hand make him highly effective against spin.
Against fast bowlers, his average is slightly lower but still respectable. Steven Smith's batting average against Pace bowling in Test matches is 37.0. He remains a key wicket for opposing pace attacks.

Section 3: One-Day International Profile
While best known for Test cricket, he has also been a key contributor in the 50-over format.
Over his career, Steven Smith's batting average in ODI matches is 45.7. This demonstrates his consistency as a middle-order accumulator in limited-overs matches.
Additionally, his scoring pace in ODIs is excellent. Steven Smith's strike rate in ODI matches is 91.9. This strike rate shows his ability to accelerate the scoring when required.

Conclusion:
Steven Smith remains a modern Test batting giant, boasting a 43.4 career average, exceptional stats against spin (60.5 average), and a solid ODI foundation with a 45.7 batting average.
